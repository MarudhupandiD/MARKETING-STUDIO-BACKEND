Copywriting Frameworks
AIDA — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action
Hook with a bold statement, build interest with benefits, create desire with proof, close with a strong CTA. Best for ads and landing pages.
PAS — Problem, Agitate, Solution
Identify the pain point, make it feel urgent, then present your product as the only logical solution. Highly effective in email marketing.
BAB — Before, After, Bridge
Paint the "before" state (struggle), show the "after" (success), then bridge the gap with your offer. Great for testimonials and case studies.
The Power of One
One big idea, one key emotion, one specific reader, one clear CTA. Focused copy always outperforms scattered messaging.
Social Proof Triggers
Numbers, reviews, and endorsements boost credibility. Always quantify: "10,000+ customers" beats "many customers" every time.
Headline Formulas That Work
"How to [benefit] without [pain]" — "The secret to [desire]" — "[Number] ways to [goal]" — "Why [common belief] is wrong"
